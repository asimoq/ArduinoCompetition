Team Jerry

About us

Our team is dedicated to the innovative design and development of Arduino-based robots for Bosch's maze-solving competition, hosted annually by our university. With a focus on functionality and efficiency, our robots aim to navigate mazes using RFID information and ultrasonic sensors to determine their position relative to the maze walls. Our website showcases the evolution of our designs, featuring both the current and previous year's robots.
